Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is carob?

Carob is a natural superfood made from pods of the carob tree (Ceratonia siliqua), native to the Mediterranean. The pods are dried, roasted, and ground into a naturally sweet, nutrient-dense powder.

Does carob taste like chocolate?

No. Carob has its own unique flavor - earthy, naturally sweet, and mildly nutty. While it looks like cocoa, it's distinctly different. We encourage you to enjoy it as its own superfood, not just as a chocolate substitute.

Is carob safe during pregnancy?

Yes! Carob is 100% caffeine-free and generally safe during pregnancy, making it a great alternative to caffeinated drinks. However, we always recommend consulting your doctor before adding any new food to your diet.

Can children consume carob?

Yes, carob is safe for children above 3 years. It's caffeine-free and contains no stimulants, making it a healthy, calm-energy drink for kids.

Why is the texture grainy?

Carob is naturally extremely high in fiber (37.9g per 100g), which is excellent for gut health. The slight graininess is a sign of this pure, minimally processed fiber. For a smoother texture, blend well or use a frother.

How much should I consume daily?

Start with 1 tablespoon (7g) daily and gradually increase to 2 tablespoons. Due to high fiber content, increase intake slowly and drink plenty of water.

What's the shelf life?

12 months from the manufacturing date when sealed. Once opened, best consumed within 3 months. Store in a cool, dry place in an airtight container.

Is it organic?

Our carob is sourced from premium farms in Spain. While not certified organic, it is lab-tested to be free from harmful pesticides and contaminants. You can view our NABL lab certificate on the homepage.

Can diabetics consume carob?

Carob contains natural sugars. While it has a lower glycemic index than refined sugar, diabetics should consult their doctor and monitor blood sugar levels.
