Simple Ways to Enjoy

Classic Carob Drink

  1. Pour 200ml milk (dairy or plant-based)
  2. Add 1 tbsp Aarasa Carob Powder
  3. Sweeten with jaggery, honey, or monk fruit
  4. Blend until smooth
  5. Enjoy warm or chilled!

Healthy Baking

  • Cookies
  • Muffins
  • Brownies
  • Energy balls & Laddoos
